FIREFOX EXTENSION RELOAD INSTRUCTIONS:

After building changes, you need to reload the extension in Firefox:

Method 1 - Temporary Extension (about:debugging):
1. Open Firefox
2. Go to: about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox
3. Click "Reload" button next to your extension

Method 2 - Full Reload:
1. Go to: about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox
2. Click "Remove" to uninstall
3. Click "Load Temporary Add-on"
4. Navigate to and select: firefox-extension/manifest.json

Method 3 - Clear Cache and Reload:
1. Close all Firefox tabs showing the extension
2. Go to about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox
3. Click "Reload"
4. Open a new tab to see changes
